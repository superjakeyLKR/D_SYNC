Project: D_SYNC

How to Play:

Find the hidden word or phrase in each puzzle and enter it into the URL to proceed.

How to Enter:

Enter the URL in the address bar of your browser after the https://superjakeylkr.github.io/D_SYNC/LEVELNAME/

Add .html to the end of the word, unless specified otherwise.

Example:
The answer is 1.
https://superjakeylkr.github.io/D_SYNC/level1/
goes to
https://superjakeylkr.github.io/D_SYNC/level1/1.html

If a page saying 404 shows up, that means that you are incorrect, or have entered the answer in incorrectly.
